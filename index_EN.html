      IT HR Schoolest 2013
      Trainings and seminars In our school we conduct one- and two-day trainings for recruiters and HR specialists of IT companies on such topics as sourcing, IT-training, benefits and compensation management, self-efficacy management. Course Comprehensive training for novice recruiters with no experience but an intense desire to master the world of IT. During the course we teach you to navigate today's IT labor market, to take into account specifics and trends in dealing with staff recruitment. We have a lot of practice and only the necessary theory. After the course, you begin to understand the software development process, the structure of IT companies and will be able to search for and find developers of different levels and profiles.

      IT-EVENTS AGGREGATOR est 2012
      On our portal any organizer can present their conference, meetup, hackathon, training, forum or other events in the area of information technology, robotics and telecom. Thanks to the aggregator IT-Events.com not a single member of the Runet IT community will miss any interesting event!
      How to add an event to the website?
      Step 1. You need to register or log in.
      Step 2. Enter your account.
      Step 3. In the panel to the right click button Add an event.
      How to get the IT-Events social media news feed?
      Step 1. Add an event to the IT-Events.com website
      Step 2. Add our logo and link IT-Events.com on the page of your event. Our logo is here.
      Step 3. Send us a link to the page of your event and a link to your event on our website by email admin@it-events.com. If all the conditions are met, we will put your event in the posting plan on our social networks.
      How to delete my event from the website?
      You need to enable edit mode for the event. Click the "Send to archive" button. Your event will become unavailable.
